# Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

> **Demo Caveat:** This file is a curated summary for demonstration purposes only. Eligibility rules,
> benefit amounts, and hospital lists change periodically. Always verify current details on the
> official portal: https://pmjay.gov.in

## Purpose
Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY is India's flagship government-funded health insurance scheme. It provides
cashless and paperless access to healthcare at empanelled hospitals for poor and vulnerable families.

## Who It Helps
- Families identified as poor or near-poor in the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data.
- Includes both rural and urban poor households.
- There is **no income-based registration** — eligibility is determined purely by SECC data.

## Eligibility Summary
- **Rural families**: those listed under SECC 2011 deprivation categories (e.g., households without a
  pucca house, daily-wage manual labourers, SC/ST families, etc.)
- **Urban families**: listed occupational categories in SECC 2011 (ragpickers, domestic workers,
  street vendors, construction workers, sanitation workers, etc.)
- Family size is not capped — the entire family is covered under one card.
- No upper age limit.

## Benefits
- Health cover of up to **₹5 lakh per family per year** for secondary and tertiary hospitalisation.
- Covers **more than 1,500 medical packages** including surgery, medical treatment, and daycare.
- **Pre- and post-hospitalisation** expenses covered (up to 3 days before, 15 days after).
- Completely **cashless and paperless** at empanelled hospitals.
- Covers **pre-existing conditions** from day one.

## Common Documents Required
- Aadhaar card (for identity verification at hospital)
- Ration card or SECC-linked family data (to verify eligibility)
- Ayushman card / e-card (generated after eligibility verification)

## How to Apply / Next Steps
1. Visit https://pmjay.gov.in or the Ayushman Bharat app to check eligibility using your mobile
   number or Aadhaar.
2. If eligible, visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or an empanelled hospital's Ayushman
   Mitra desk to generate your Ayushman card.
3. Use the card at any empanelled public or private hospital for cashless treatment.
4. The National Health Authority (NHA) helpline is **14555**.
