# PM-KISAN – Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi

> **Demo Caveat:** This file is a curated summary for demonstration purposes only. Benefit amounts,
> eligibility rules, and instalment schedules may change. Always verify on the official portal:
> https://pmkisan.gov.in

## Purpose
PM-KISAN provides direct income support to small and marginal farmers across India. The scheme
transfers cash directly into farmers' bank accounts to supplement their financial needs related to
crop inputs and household expenses.

## Who It Helps
- Small and marginal farmer families who own cultivable land.
- Covers landholding farmers throughout the country, both in rural and urban areas.

## Eligibility Summary
- Farmer families with **cultivable landholding** in their name (as per land records).
- Family means the husband, wife, and minor children.
- **Excluded categories:**
  - Institutional landholders (trusts, companies, etc.)
  - Former and current holders of constitutional posts
  - Former and serving state/central government employees (except multi-tasking staff, peons, and
    Class IV employees)
  - Professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants) registered with professional
    bodies
  - Families paying income tax in the last assessment year
  - Retired pensioners drawing a monthly pension of ₹10,000 or more

## Benefits
- **₹6,000 per year** paid directly to the farmer's bank account.
- Paid in **three equal instalments of ₹2,000 each**, released every four months.
- Transferred via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) — no middlemen.

## Common Documents Required
- Aadhaar card
- Land ownership/land record documents (Khasra / Khatauni)
- Bank account details linked to Aadhaar
- Mobile number

## How to Apply / Next Steps
1. Visit https://pmkisan.gov.in and use the **Farmer Corner** section to self-register.
2. Alternatively, approach the local Patwari (revenue officer), Common Service Centre (CSC), or
   state government agriculture office.
3. After registration, check your beneficiary status on the portal using Aadhaar number or mobile
   number.
4. Helpline: **155261** or **011-23381092**
