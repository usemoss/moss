# PM Vishwakarma Yojana

> tool kits, loan limits, and registration processes may be updated by the government. Always verify
> details at: https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in

## Purpose
PM Vishwakarma is a Central Sector Scheme launched in 2023 to provide holistic support to
artisans and craftspeople (called "Vishwakarmas") who work with their hands and tools. The scheme
recognises traditional skills and aims to improve the quality of products, income, and market
reach of these workers.

## Who It Helps
- Traditional artisans and craftspeople working in 18 trade categories, including:
  - Carpenter (Suthar / Badhai)
  - Boat maker
  - Armourer
  - Blacksmith (Lohar)
  - Hammer and tool kit maker
  - Locksmith
  - Goldsmith (Sonar)
  - Potter (Kumhaar)
  - Sculptor / Stone carver / Stone breaker
  - Cobbler / Shoemaker (Charmkar)
  - Mason (Rajmistri)
  - Basket / Mat / Broom maker / Coir weaver
  - Doll / Toy maker (traditional)
  - Barber (Naai)
  - Garland maker (Malakaar)
  - Washerman (Dhobi)
  - Tailor (Darzi)
  - Fishing net maker

## Eligibility Summary
- Must be working in one of the 18 designated trades using hands and tools.
- Must be 18 years or older at the time of registration.
- Must be self-employed / engaged in the unorganised sector.
- One member per family is eligible.
- Should not have taken loans under PM SVANidhi, MUDRA, or similar government schemes in the
  previous 5 years (some exceptions may apply — check portal).
- Artisans enrolled in PM JANMAN or Forest Rights Act beneficiaries may also be eligible.

## Benefits
- **Recognition**: PM Vishwakarma Certificate and ID card.
- **Skill Upgrade**: Free basic training (5 days) and advanced training (15+ days) with a stipend
  of ₹500 per day during training.
- **Toolkit Incentive**: ₹15,000 e-voucher to buy modern tools.
- **Collateral-free loans**:
  - First tranche: up to ₹1 lakh at a concessional interest rate of 5%.
  - Second tranche: up to ₹2 lakh (after satisfactory repayment of first loan).
- **Digital payment incentive**: ₹1 per transaction (up to 100 per month) for accepting digital payments.
- **Marketing support**: assistance with branding, quality certification, and linking to national
  marketplaces like GeM and retail outlets.

## Common Documents Required
- Aadhaar card
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
- Bank account details
- Ration card or family details
- Self-declaration about trade and self-employment

## How to Apply / Next Steps
1. Visit https://pmvishwakarma.gov.in or the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC).
2. Registration is done by CSC Village Level Entrepreneurs (VLEs) using biometric verification.
3. After registration, a PM Vishwakarma ID card and certificate are issued.
4. Skill training is arranged through nearest training centres.
5. Toolkit e-voucher and loans are processed after training completion.
6. Helpline: **18002677777**
